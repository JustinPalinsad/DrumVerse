 DrumVerse – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/14/2024

DrumVerse respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic gameplay data such as scores, levels, and settings.

We may collect device information like your device type and OS version.

If the game uses ads or analytics, those services may collect anonymous usage data.

We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identity unless you choose to share it.

2. How We Use the Information

To improve gameplay and app performance

To fix bugs

To understand how players use the game

To show ads (if enabled)

We do not sell your data.

3. Children’s Privacy

We do not collect personal information from children.
If something was shared by mistake, contact us so we can delete it.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.
